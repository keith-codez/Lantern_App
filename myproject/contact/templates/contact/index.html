Website VS Web App

Not sure what you need? Let’s break it down for you!

What’s a Website?

A website is like a beautifully designed brochure or gallery—perfect for sharing information, showcasing your services, and building your online presence.

  • Static content (like a digital storefront)
  • Accessible to anyone
  • Focus on information & visuals

What’s a Web App?

A web app is like a digital tool or platform—interactive, dynamic, and built to solve problems or engage users in exciting ways.

  • Interactive & user-focused
  • Customizable experiences
  • Think of apps like Airbnb or Amazon

Whether you need a stunning website to tell your story or a powerful web app to engage users, we’ve got you covered!
